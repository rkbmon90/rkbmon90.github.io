Short Bio

Rakib Mondal is a researcher in applied mathematics with a primary focus on the analysis of partial differential equations. His work emphasizes hyperbolic conservation laws, nonlinear wave phenomena, and related mathematical models arising in fluid dynamics and continuum mechanics.
