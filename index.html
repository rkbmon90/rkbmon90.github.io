Short Bio

Welcome to personal homepage of Rakib Mondal. Rakib Mondal is a researcher in mathematics with a primary focus on the analysis of partial differential equations. His work emphasizes hyperbolic conservation/balance laws, nonlinear wave phenomena, and related mathematical models arising in fluid dynamics and continuum mechanics.
